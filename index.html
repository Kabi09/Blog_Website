Thai Pongal

Date:14-01-2024

Thai Pongal is a Tamil harvest festival honoring the Sun God, celebrated with the preparation of a dish called "Pongal" made from fresh rice, jaggery, and lentils. Homes are adorned with kolam (rangoli), prayers are offered, and families come together to celebrate.

Mattu Pongal

Date:15-01-2024

Mattu Pongal, celebrated the day after Thai Pongal, is dedicated to honoring cattle, which play a crucial role in agriculture. Cows and bulls are bathed, decorated with garlands, and worshipped, reflecting their importance in farming. This day also includes cultural activities and a sense of community among farmers.

Kaanum Pongal

Date:16-01-2024

Kaanum Pongal, the last day of the Pongal festival, is a day for socializing and spending time with loved ones. Families visit relatives, share meals, and enjoy outings or picnics. Elders give blessings to the younger generation, and people often visit scenic spots or beaches. It marks a joyful conclusion to the harvest celebrations.

Republic Day (India)

Date:26-02-2024

Republic Day in India commemorates the adoption of the Constitution on January 26, 1950, marking the nation's transition to a republic.
